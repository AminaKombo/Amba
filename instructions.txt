Installation:

1. Set Up a Database:

    - Open app/data/base.sql in a text editor
    - Change database name (if you'd like)
    - Import base.sql into your server (or copy/paste it into the GUI)

2. Configure your Application:

    - Open app/config/config.php in a text editor
    - Change the constants to your preferred settings
    - Open the URL you specified in the AMBALINK constant
 
 
 Notes:
 
 1. Your system must be configured to allow outgoing emails
